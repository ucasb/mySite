Barbie (2023) Miniature Set

Barbie World Premier - Shrine Auditorium and Expo Hall, Los Angeles CA

A fun little scale model of Barbie's dream house from the recent film - luckily it was a dead simple set. Each floor is two layers of 3/4" MDF with CNC'd reliefs that provide clearance for bolts that capture the steel support rods. All parts finished with a very high gloss Barbie-pink.

I also designed the two large "B" icons that top the miniature and disco ball.
