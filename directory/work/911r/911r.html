1967 Porsche 911 R Lens Repro

TRE Motorsports - Los Angeles, CA

This project is the how and why I learned to cast and design molds. Luckily, I was working as a lab manager at the time, and had all the tools lying around in different corners of the factory. Initially, I planned on SLA-printing all 25 lenses, but soon learned the upper limits of photopolymer resin transparency.

In the end, I produced over several molds, 25 lenses and lens seals, plus a vacuum forming jig for the lens housing, and a forming jig for bending the lenses into shape.
