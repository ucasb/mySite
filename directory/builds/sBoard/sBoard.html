SDRE 'Diary' Skateboard

Deck built from scratch, painted to resemble the cover of Sunny Day Real Estates 1994 album, Diary.

The steps can be found here and here, but essentially, 7 layers of maple veneer are laid up and glued in perpendicular orientation, placed in a vacuum bag, and shaped around a carved foam mold. After drying, a shape is jigsawed out, painted, and finished.

I'll be the first to admit I'm no painter, so I used a laser cutter and painters tape to perfectly mask off the vectorized design, which worked pretty well.

Current components are the cheap ones I had on hand, I plan on replacing the trucks and wheels are some point.

Last photo shows the camper skateboards made during BitSpace's week-long 2019 summer skateboard camp. Please note the amazing Piranha plant, Final Fantasy Cloud sword and TWO Jojo stands - the kids are alright.
