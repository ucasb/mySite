1994 Zaskar LE

Frame up build based on a 1994 GT Zaskar LE with the aqua anodization. Frame was raced by its original owner and shows it with a ton of wear, so I get to ride it guilt-free. Highlights include:

  • Chris King headset
  • Mavic x517 rims laced to DT Swiss 240 hubs with Maxxis Oriflamme tires
  • XTR grouppo, minus the XT front mech
  • KORE lite post
  • Syncros hinged stem
  • Brooks c17 saddle
  • Azonic MTN-X pedals (replaced MKS pedals pictured)
  • RockShox SID XC fork with carbon one-piece steerer/crown
  • Mystery superlight alloy riser bars
